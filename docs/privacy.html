Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Amena ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Christian prayer app developed by Louis Blankaert. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use Amena.

1. Data We Collect

Amena is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All your data (name, prayer times, prayer history, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults).

2. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect anonymous usage data such as app opens, prayer completions, and onboarding steps. This data helps us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included. You can learn more at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

3. AI-Generated Prayers

Amena uses the Google Gemini API to generate personalized prayers. When a prayer is generated, a prompt describing your prayer preferences (time of day, general mood) is sent to Google's API. No personal identifiers (name, email, etc.) are included in these requests. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Subscriptions

Amena offers auto-renewable subscriptions (weekly and yearly) managed entirely by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information. Apple handles all billing. See Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

5. Notifications

With your permission, Amena sends local push notifications to remind you to pray. These notifications are generated on your device and are never sent to our servers.

6. No Account Required

Amena does not require you to create an account. There is no login, no email, no password.

7. Children's Privacy

Amena is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: blankaertlouis@outlook.com
